Kutaisi Walking Tour

Kutaisi Walking Tour with wine tasting

Do you have an extra hour in the city? Explore Kutaisi with our walking tour and taste natural Georgian wine. The tour covers central square, royal district of Kutaisi, Bazar and etc. Follow us to explore Kutaisi.

Details of tour:

  • Season - Full year
  • Type – Culture and History
  • Duration – 1 hour
  • Frequency – Every day

Highlights:

  • Central square and Kolkheti fountain
  • Opera house
  • White bridge
  • Park of love
  • Golden Marquee palace
  • Central Bazar of Kutaisi
  • Glass of fine Georgian wine

Tour starts at 12:00 from our office in Kutaisi. Here guide tells about history of Georgia and Kutaisi. After taking pictures at fountain group continues down to central park where are located statue of sister “Ishkhnelebi” and memorial of “9 April”. Afterwards we pass by Kutaisi city hall, Opera house and visit “Mon Plasir” arch. Then we pass by the first gymnasium and visit white bridge, then go through park of love and visit Golden marquee palace. Then we go to central grocery market called “Mtsvane Bazari”. After the market we walk through the central park of Kutaisi and arrive on central square of the city. In the end of the tour we provide a glass of fine Georgian wine in the wine bar Amber Wine. The tour finishes at our office in Kutaisi.

Price

Group tour - 20 GEL per person
Private tour - from 80 GEL
